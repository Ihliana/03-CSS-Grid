The ways to use the sun's energy.

Every day, the sun radiates a thousand times more heat onto the world’s deserts than we use. Solar technology can convert that energy into steam, and then electricity. We could meet the entire world’s energy needs by using just one per cent of total landmass for solar energy.

To grow your food.

Take away the sun, and what can you grow? With just soil and sunlight you can grow tomatoes, peppers, apples, and much more.

To generate electricity

It won’t be long before most roofs are covered with solar panels.

To design a house

When an architect designs a passive solar home, they make the most of the sun’s light and heat by using south facing windows, maximizing insulation on the north, and creating a thermal mass to store solar heat.
